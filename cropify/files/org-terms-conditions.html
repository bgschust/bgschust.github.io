Pay at Pick-Up (USD)

The default payment method is for customers to pay at pick-up in U.S. dollars. Be sure to collect this payment when the customer picks up their order.

Currency Considerations

Calculated Pricing

The USD prices on your market items update when you set AgG prices on the sell-item page. Press enter in the "Price (AgG)" numeric input box to see the "Price (USD)" locked input change. The USD price will update again when it is re-calculated for a customer at checkout, and this price is shown in their order history and your sale history.

Exchange Rates

Exchange rates between USD and AgG are found by taking the lowest bulk price for transaction tokens on a USD per AgG basis.
